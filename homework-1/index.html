About Me

Influenced by Brazil's huge advertising scene, Isabella traveled to the US six years ago in search of more inspiration. She is eager to learn and constantly seeking creative escapades to keep herself stimulated. Her dream is to work for a talented and passionate team at one of Charlotte's local ad agencies.

Motived by the huge demand on the web development field, Isabella is now pursuing a certification in full stack development. She intends to combine her degree and experience in graphic design with the new web development skills to evolve with the industry.
